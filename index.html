ROS2 Navigation Stack — Design & Deployment

Braintree Technologies · Robotics Software Engineer
2021 — PRESENT
Designed and deployed a full ROS2 Nav2-based navigation system for agricultural robots operating in oil-palm and chilli farm environments. Built a pointcloud-fusion pipeline with PCL, integrating Livox Horizon and Velodyne LiDAR — voxel-grid downsampling, ROI segmentation, and Euclidean clustering for robust obstacle detection.
  • Customized Nav2 global/local planners and optimized Costmap2D layers for real-time dense terrain
  • ROS1 → ROS2 migration: launch files, URDF/Xacro, plugins, Gazebo workflows
  • Custom ROS plugins, MoveIt arm integration, and object-detection perception pipelines
  • Hardware bring-up: RealSense, Jetson, Arduino, Teensy — drivers, networking, deployment
  • Validated through extensive field testing across varied outdoor conditions
